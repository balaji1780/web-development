About Bharatheeyudu

"Bharatheeyudu" is a 1996 Indian Tamil-language vigilante action film directed by S. Shankar. It features Kamal Haasan in dual roles as both a freedom fighter and his son. The film highlights themes of corruption and the pursuit of justice.

Known for its compelling narrative and gripping performances, "Bharatheeyudu" remains a cinematic classic, earning critical acclaim and numerous accolades, including the National Film Award for Best Actor.
